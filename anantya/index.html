ABOUT

Anantya is an intercollegiate event for undergraduate students organized by the Department of Computer Engineering, PCCoE.

Consisting of 14 technical and non-technical events, Anantya is held to encourage students to create, hack, innovate and show off their talents. It offers students a platform to learn, grow and earn exciting prizes along the way.

To get more information about Anantya check out our brochure.
