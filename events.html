PCCOE ACM

Set up in 2012, the PCCOE ACM Student Chapter is one of the best Student Chapter in Pune. Year by year, the Chapter strives to increase the number of events, and the participation of students. With the guidance of the faculty sponsors, and the enthusiastic volunteer and participating students, the Chapter which is now in its Eleventh year is improving rapidly on all counts.
